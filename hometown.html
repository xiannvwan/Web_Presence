My hometown

Tong Hua—city of wine

Tong Hua, a city in southern Jilin province, has a tradition of wine-making. In 1937, the first wine factory in China was built there and its wine, together with Mao-tai -- one of the most popular and famous wine brands in China, was the wine chosen for the founding ceremony of the People's Republic of China in 1949. Then Premier Zhou Enlai named it the Wine for National Day Celebration. After many years ‘efforts, its two wines Tong Hua and Tongtian are now renowned Chinese brands. Tong Hua, with its unique climate and advantageous geographical conditions, is perfect for grapes.
